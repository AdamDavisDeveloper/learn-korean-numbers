learn the korean number systems

Here are the Sino Korean numbers 1-10 with their romanizations.

1: 일 (il)
2: 이 (i)
3: 삼 (sam)
4: 사 (sa)
5: 오 (o)
6: 륙 (ryuk) or 육 (yuk)
7: 칠 (chil)
8: 팔 (pal)
9: 구 (gu)
10: 십 (sip)

then we have 10, 100, 1000, and 10,000

100: 백 (baek)
1000: 천 (cheon)
10,000: 만 (man)
